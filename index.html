Welcome to GOLD WASTE

GOLD WASTE

Solution to waste while providing wealth

Gold Waste Limited is a forward thinking environmental service provider with experience, expertise and resources

required to provide long-term lasting solution to problem of waste disposal and pollution by collecting and recycling waste.

to create wealth and resources for individuals in Nigeria.

Core business activities.

1. collection and disposal of house hold, commercial and industrial wastes.

2. Buying of non-consumables like metals, aluminium and plastic from individuals and companies.

3. Recycling.

4. Litter and vegetation management services.

5. Land fill management.

6. Sale, hiring & leasing of waste management trucks and trash cans and buckets.

Vision

To be the best waste management company and to create wealth,

resources and healthy living from waste disposals and recycling

Mission

Creating a better clean and safe environment through cutting edge professional service delivery.

Pushing boundaries of waste management benefits in Nigeria and the outside world.
