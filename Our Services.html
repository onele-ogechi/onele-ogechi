OUR SERVICES

Residential/Commercial Services

Our company provides prompt and effective waste collection and disposal services to residential apartments, hotels, restaurants, banks, lock-up shops and offices in Lagos.

Industrial Services.

We provide waste collection and waste disposal services to industries, construction and demolition sites, shopping malls and markets in Lagos and the Frederal Capital Territory Abuja.

We make use of specialized mordern wate collection equipment such as Roll-On-Roll-Off and Skip-Bin for collection and disposal of construction/demolition wastes as well as other specilized waste.

Leasing Of Mordern Waste Trucks.

Gold Waste also specializes in importation and supply of waste management equipments and trucks to Nigeria. We supply waste collection and disposal trucks such as compactors, roll-on-roll-off and skip bin trucks, waste transfer station, ejector bulk haulage trailers, waste bins and containers as well as waste recycling equipment.

Gold Waste import trucks and supply to waste management agencies at the state levels, federal level and private waste management operators accross Nigeria.
